Visitor Guidelines

Kakum National Park

  • Stay on marked trails to protect the forest ecosystem.
  • Do not feed wildlife; it disrupts their natural behavior.
  • Book canopy walkway tours in advance due to limited slots.
  • Wear sturdy shoes and insect repellent.
  • Respect quiet zones for birdwatching.

Bui National Park

  • Follow boat safety guidelines during river cruises.
  • Avoid swimming near hippo or crocodile habitats.
  • Carry binoculars for wildlife spotting.
  • Dispose of waste responsibly to protect the river.
  • Check dam-related updates before visiting.

Mole National Park

  • Stay with guides during walking safaris for safety.
  • Keep a safe distance from elephants and other wildlife.
  • Bring sun protection and water for long treks.
  • No flash photography near animals.
  • Respect park curfew times.

Bia National Park

  • Obtain a permit for forest walks.
  • Do not disturb research sites or equipment.
  • Wear long sleeves to avoid insects.
  • Travel in groups for safety.
  • Leave no trace of your visit.

Ankasa National Park

  • Prepare for wet conditions with waterproof gear.
  • Follow guide instructions in dense forest areas.
  • Avoid loud noises to protect wildlife.
  • Carry a first-aid kit for emergencies.
  • Register at the park entrance.

Boabeng-Fiema Monkey Sanctuary

  • Respect the monkeys as sacred; do not chase them.
  • Follow cultural protocols during village tours.
  • Keep food secured to avoid attracting monkeys.
  • Stay on designated paths.
  • Support local guides for a richer experience.

Shai Resource Reserve

  • Wear comfortable shoes for cave explorations.
  • Do not disturb historical cave artifacts.
  • Keep vehicles on designated roads.
  • Bring water and hats for sunny days.
  • Follow ranger instructions during game drives.

Tafi Atome Monkey Sanctuary

  • Do not feed or touch the monkeys.
  • Participate in community-led tours only.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Bring a camera for photography (no flash).
  • Leave pets at home to avoid conflicts.

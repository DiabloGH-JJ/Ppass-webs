Shai Resource Reserve

Shai Resource Reserve, in Ghana’s Greater Accra Region, is a scenic park established in 1962. It features rolling hills and diverse wildlife, ideal for safari drives.

Main Details

  • Size: 47 km²
  • Ecosystem: Savannah and Woodland
  • Wildlife Highlights: Kob antelopes, monkeys, and birds
  • Famous For: Rolling hills and safari drives

Wildlife Highlights

  • Kob antelopes and monkeys
  • Various bird species
  • Small mammals like hares
  • Rich grassland flora

Activities

  • Safari drives
  • Guided walks
  • Birdwatching
  • Picnicking

Fun Facts

  • It features ancient caves used by the Shai people!
  • Established in 1962 as a game production reserve.
  • Home to over 150 bird species.

Tafi Atome Sanctuary

Tafi Atome Sanctuary, in Ghana’s Volta Region, is a community-managed reserve established to protect its monkey population. It’s famous for its friendly monkeys and cultural interactions.

Main Details

  • Size: 5 km²
  • Ecosystem: Tropical Forest
  • Wildlife Highlights: Mona monkeys, colobus monkeys
  • Famous For: Monkey interactions and community tourism

Wildlife Highlights

  • Mona monkeys and colobus monkeys
  • Small birds and reptiles
  • Local flora
  • Butterfly species

Activities

  • Monkey interaction tours
  • Cultural village visits
  • Forest walks
  • Photography

Fun Facts

  • Monkeys here are fed by locals as part of tradition!
  • It’s a community-managed sanctuary since the 1990s.
  • Visitors can hear traditional storytelling.
